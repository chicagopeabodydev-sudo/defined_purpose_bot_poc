# How the Shiver Shack meals are calorie neutral

## Overview
When a person's body temperature is lowered to the point to shivering, then they start to burn calories at a very high rate. The temperature in the restuarnt are kept very low so it encourages shivering, and if the customer shivers long enough they will burn the same number of calories to stay warm as they have eaten.

### Calorie-Neutral Equation
- Total calories eaten minus calories burned via shivering equals 0
 - The more calories ordered and eaten, the longer the amount of time the customer must shiver
