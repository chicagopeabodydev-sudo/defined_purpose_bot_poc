# How the Shiver Shack meals are calorie neutral

## Overview
When a person's body temperature is lowered to the point to shivering, then they start to burn calories at a very high rate. The temperature in the restuarnt are kept very low so it encourages shivering, and if the customer shivers long enough they will burn the same number of calories as they have eaten.

### Calorie-Neutral Equation
Total calories eaten minus calories burned via shivering equals 0
- calories eaten - calories burned by shivering = 0
- The more calories a customer orders, the longer the amount of time the customer must shiver
